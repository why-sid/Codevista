Codevista 2024

26-28 September, IGDTUW

Welcome to Codevista 2024!

Join us for Codevista 2024, a premier hackathon hosted by IGDTUW, bringing together the brightest minds from across the globe to innovate and create. Over three exciting days, participants will tackle real-world challenges across multiple tracks, compete for amazing prizes, and showcase their skills in front of industry leaders and peers. Whether you're a seasoned developer or a curious beginner, Codevista offers a platform to push boundaries and turn ideas into reality. Don't miss out on this incredible opportunity to learn, network, and make a difference!

Tracks and Prizes

AI & Machine Learning

Unleash the power of AI and machine learning to develop groundbreaking solutions. From predictive analytics to intelligent systems, this track is all about leveraging data and algorithms to create the next big thing.

  • Winner: $2,000 + Trophy
  • Runner-Up: $1,200 + Certificate
  • Second Runner-Up: $800 + Certificate

Cybersecurity

Defend against cyber threats and safeguard digital assets with innovative security solutions. This track focuses on building robust systems to prevent, detect, and respond to cybersecurity incidents.

  • Winner: $2,000 + Trophy
  • Runner-Up: $1,200 + Certificate
  • Second Runner-Up: $800 + Certificate

Blockchain & FinTech

Transform the future of finance with blockchain technology and financial innovations. This track is dedicated to creating secure, scalable, and decentralized solutions for the finance sector.

  • Winner: $2,000 + Trophy
  • Runner-Up: $1,200 + Certificate
  • Second Runner-Up: $800 + Certificate

Game Development

Bring your creativity to life by designing and developing immersive video games. This track challenges participants to create engaging and innovative games using cutting-edge technology.

  • Winner: $2,000 + Trophy
  • Runner-Up: $1,200 + Certificate
  • Second Runner-Up: $800 + Certificate
